Security

Your keys never leave
your device.

AES-GCM vault

Keys encrypted with PBKDF2-derived key. Zero plaintext ever written to disk.

Session lock

Auto-locks on browser close or configurable idle timeout. Password required to resume.

AI sees no secrets

The LLM only receives addresses, token balances, and contacts — never private keys or seeds.

Explicit approval

Every dApp connection, transaction, and message sign triggers a dedicated approval popup.

⚠ Experimental software — do not store significant funds.
